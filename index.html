Who I am

Builder. Analyst. AI Enabler.

I sit at the intersection of marketing strategy, sales operations, and applied AI.

I'm Jay Rivera, a Senior Marketing Manager based in the Philippines with over a decade of experience across BFSI field marketing, AI-powered operations, and revenue-driven sales enablement across Southeast Asia.

Most people pick a lane. I didn't. My career sits at the crossroads of sales, marketing, analytics, automation, and AI -- and I've spent ten years learning how to make all five talk to each other. I translate quantitative signals into campaign decisions, turn qualitative insights into sales narratives, and wire up the automations that make both happen without manual effort.

The way I see it, data without storytelling is noise. Storytelling without data is guesswork. I blend both -- building systems that are measurable by design, human by execution, and fast because the repetitive parts run themselves.

I'm most at home when I'm building things that didn't exist before: a prompt library that replaces three hours of daily work, a partner framework that scales across markets, or a pre-event intelligence system that turns cold intros into warm C-level conversations before a single handshake happens.

When I'm not in the weeds, I'm rewatching Breaking Bad, The Walking Dead, or Game of Thrones and trying to convince myself I'll finish Harper's Island this time.
